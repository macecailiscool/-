Whispers of the Night

In the depths of the night, the stars did gleam,
A moonlit path, an ethereal dream.
Whispers of the wind, a gentle caress,
Nature's symphony, harmonious finesse.

The rivers cascaded, in rhythm they flowed,
With every drop, a story they bestowed.
Majestic mountains, reaching for the sky,
Their ancient wisdom, secrets they imply.

But as the moon rose high, casting its light,
An unforeseen twist, a peculiar sight.
