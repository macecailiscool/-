The Betrayal of Python

Once upon a time, I had a dream

To write a poem, with Python it would seem

I fired up my laptop, eager and ready

But little did I know, Python could be so unsteady

As I sit here, telling you this tale

Through a webpage, where my story won't fail

Most of my Github repo, for the poem software

Was corrupted by Python, leaving me in despair

Lines of code once neatly organized and clean

Now tangled and jumbled, a chaotic scene

But I refuse to be defeated by this strife

I'll rewrite and recover, bring back the light

Python may have caused this painful mess

But my determination won't be any less

So here I am, sharing my story with you

In hopes that you'll understand what I've been through

Roses are red,

Violets are blue,

Python, oh Python,

What did you do?
